NextGen Membership and Fellowship Program

Unlock Opportunities, Build Connections, and Lead the Future.

Our membership and fellowship programs are designed to empower young achievers to dream big, connect deeply, and grow exponentially.

Fellowship Program

Become a NextGen Fellow: Lead, Inspire, and Partner.

The NextGen Fellowship is for exceptional individuals ready to take their influence to the next level. Fellows are inducted yearly and gain exclusive opportunities to become individual partners of the organization, amplifying their impact within and beyond NextGen.

Fellowship Perks:

  • Yearly induction into the distinguished NextGen Fellows Circle.
  • Exclusive invitations to global events and opportunities to represent NextGen on international stages.
  • Mentorship from world-class leaders to sharpen your leadership and vision.
  • Opportunities to design and lead impactful projects within NextGen.
  • Recognition as an individual partner of NextGen, opening doors to greater influence and collaborations.
Annual Fee:

Fellows pay an annual subscription fee to maintain their status, with access to all fellowship benefits.

Why Join NextGen?

  • Build Connections: Network with ambassadors, youth leaders, and industry icons who can guide your journey.
  • Explore Global Opportunities: Priority access to scholarships, internships, and international trips.
  • Learn and Lead: Participate in transformative training sessions to sharpen your skills and grow your impact.
  • Be Recognized: Fellows gain leadership opportunities and the chance to shape the future of NextGen.

How to Get Started

Choose your path: Membership or Fellowship.

Complete your registration and join a network of trailblazers shaping the future. Fellows will be contacted with details of their induction after registration.
